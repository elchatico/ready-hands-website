Who We Are

Ready Hands Initiative is a youth-led first aid training organization based in Kenya. Our mission is to equip students with real-life emergency response skills that save lives — one classroom, one session, one brave student at a time.

Our Vision

To empower a generation of confident, skilled young responders who can take charge during emergencies at home, school, or in the community.

Our Journey

Born from a passion to create change, Ready Hands Initiative began as an idea during paramedic training. Today, it’s a growing movement of education, action, and student-led safety across Kenya.
