About Us

Ready Hands Initiative is a youth-led first aid training program based in Kenya. We teach students practical emergency response skills through school club sessions.

Our Program

We offer a 6-week training course covering first aid basics, bleeding, burns, CPR, safety planning, and more. Each session is interactive and hands-on.
